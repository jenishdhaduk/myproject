    COVID-19

    May 25, 2021 -- United Airlines announced on Monday that it will offer the chance to win free flights to customers who have been vaccinated against COVID-19. New or existing members of United’s MileagePlus loyalty program will be able to upload their vaccination records to the airline’s mobile app by June 22 to enter the lottery. “We’re proud to do our part to incentivize people to get their shot,” Scott Kirby, the CEO for United, said in a statement. “Thanks to the vaccine, more and more destinations are opening up for travel -- and we know our customers are eager to fly,” he said. “We’re excited to give people one more reason to get vaccinated so they can reunite with friends and family or take that long-awaited vacation, which all could be just one shot away.” .
